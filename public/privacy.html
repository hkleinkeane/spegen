SpeGen Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

SpeGen is a free, open-source augmentative and alternative communication (AAC) app. It has no user accounts and does not collect personal information for advertising or tracking. Your boards, settings, and content stay on your device. This policy explains the few cases where data leaves your device, and why.

Information stored on your device

Everything you create or configure — boards and symbols, settings, custom images, audio recordings, word-prediction history, and (if you set one) a security PIN stored only as a salted cryptographic hash — is saved in the app's local storage on your device. It is never sent to us or to any server. You can export it as a backup file or erase it at any time (Settings → Data → Restore defaults, or by uninstalling the app).

Permissions

  • Microphone — used only when you choose to record custom audio for a symbol. Recordings are saved locally on your device.
  • Photos — used only when you choose a custom image for a symbol. The selected image is copied into the app's local storage on your device.

Network services

A few features connect to third-party services to function:

  • Symbol images — to show and search picture symbols, the app sends the symbol's name or your search term to the OpenSymbols image service (opensymbols.org), routed through our proxy at spegen.vercel.app, which forwards the request and does not store it. No personal data is sent.
  • Translation — if you add a non-English spoken language, item labels are sent to Google's translation service so they can be spoken in that language. Only the label text is sent.
  • Feedback form — if you use the in-app feedback form, the title and message you type are submitted to the project's public issue tracker on GitHub. Anything you enter there becomes publicly visible, so please do not include sensitive personal information.
  • Text-to-speech uses your device's built-in speech engine; the text is processed on your device.

What we don't do

No accounts, no advertising, no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs, and we never sell your data.

Children

SpeGen is built for communication, including by children and people with disabilities. It does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, and all content stays on the device.

Data retention and your control

Your data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Backup files you export are under your control. Because there is no account, there is no server-side data for us to retain or delete.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version always lives at spegen.vercel.app/privacy.html.
